What We Offer

10 Pillars of WMAAS

A comprehensive, end-to-end accounting and finance function — from business setup to year-end closure, all under one managed service.

01

Business Setup & Financial Foundation

WMAAS helps you build your business on the right foundation. We manage company incorporation, GST registration, PAN/TAN setup, and startup registrations. Our experts guide you in selecting the ideal business structure and establish robust financial and compliance frameworks — ensuring your business is scalable and investor-ready from day one.

02

Continuous Bookkeeping & Smart Accounting

With WMAAS, your accounting is always up-to-date and audit-ready. We handle day-to-day bookkeeping, accounts payable and receivable, bank reconciliations, and ledger management using modern tools. Our structured approach provides complete financial clarity and control.

03

Integrated Taxation & Compliance

WMAAS embeds tax compliance into your daily operations. We manage GST filings, income tax returns, and TDS compliance while offering proactive tax planning strategies. Our approach ensures you remain compliant while optimizing tax efficiency.

04

Payroll & Workforce Compliance

Leveraging Work Match's Talent Solutions expertise, WMAAS delivers seamless payroll management. We manage salary processing, payslip generation, and statutory compliance including PF, ESI, and professional tax — ensuring accurate and compliant workforce financial operations.

05

Real-Time Financial Reporting & Insights

WMAAS transforms financial data into actionable insights. We provide Profit & Loss statements, Balance Sheets, Cash Flow reports, and MIS dashboards, along with budgeting and forecasting. This enables leadership teams to track performance and make informed decisions.

06

Audit-Ready Systems & Financial Governance

Be audit-ready at all times with WMAAS. We support internal and statutory audits by maintaining organized financial records, preparing documentation, and ensuring compliance. Our services strengthen governance and reduce financial risks.

07

ROC & Regulatory Compliance Management

WMAAS ensures continuous regulatory compliance. We handle ROC filings, annual compliance, director KYC, and event-based filings. Our proactive management keeps your business compliant and penalty-free.

08

Virtual CFO & Strategic Advisory

WMAAS goes beyond accounting to deliver strategic value. Our Virtual CFO services include financial planning, cost optimization, fundraising support, and performance monitoring — helping you scale with confidence and clarity.

09

Year-End Closure & Financial Finalization

We ensure seamless financial closure under WMAAS. Our team manages book closing, reconciliations, and finalization of financial statements, including Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss, along with tax provisioning and audit readiness.

10

Technology-Enabled Accounting Ecosystem

WMAAS is powered by a digital-first approach. We implement and manage platforms like Tally, Zoho Books, and QuickBooks, enabling automation, integration, and real-time reporting — making your finance function efficient and future-ready.
